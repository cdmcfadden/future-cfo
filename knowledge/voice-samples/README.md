# Voice samples

Drop excerpts of your own writing here — the more unedited the better.
Drafts, LinkedIn posts, internal memos, email rants, talk transcripts.
Claude calibrates voice off these: sentence length, cadence, vocabulary,
the specific ways you hedge and the ways you don't.

## Format

```markdown
---
title: "Optional label (e.g. '2024 year-in-review — draft')"
---

Paste the text. No structure required. Do not edit for grammar.
```

## What works

- 500–2000 words per sample is the sweet spot — enough signal, not so much
  the bundle balloons.
- **Variety beats volume.** A short angry Slack rant, a reflective essay,
  and a crisp memo teach the model more about you than three polished blog
  posts.
- Include things you wrote that you'd *never publish* — those usually have
  the most voice per word.

## What not to paste

- Anything confidential. This file ships to the model every request.
- Content that isn't yours — this folder is for voice calibration, not
  book notes. Those go in `knowledge/books/`.

This `README.md` is ignored by the loader.
